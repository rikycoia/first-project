MATERIALSPEDIA

About us

MaterialsPedia is a new project that aims to raise awareness of the history of raw materials and their intrinsic effect they have had on the development of human civilization.

The knowledge of these raw materials can be useful to understand the importance they have had in the past ... and not only that!

Desertification, deforestation, rising temperatures, wars ... are just some of the consequences we face today. For this reason it is important to find out about new technologies that can help us to have a more sustainable economy and a more correct distribution of resources. Maybe learning from the past.

It doesn't seem to be the best, but...
